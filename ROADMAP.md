# Roadmap

This document tracks the direction of the HyperSentry Labs organization
itself — not any single project. Roadmaps for individual projects, where
maintained, live in that project's own repository.

## Principles

Priorities are guided by:

- Stability and maintainability of existing projects before new ones
- Clear, complete documentation for anything that ships
- Consistent tooling and conventions across repositories, maintained through
  this `.github` repository

## Current focus

- Establishing consistent community health files, issue templates, and CI
  workflows across all organization repositories
- Improving documentation coverage for existing projects
- Refining CI and release automation shared across repositories

## How this roadmap is maintained

This roadmap reflects organization-level priorities and is updated as those
priorities change. It is not a commitment to specific dates or deliverables.
Suggestions for organization-wide direction are welcome via
[Discussions](https://github.com/orgs/hypersentry-labs/discussions/categories/ideas).
